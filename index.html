Our Project

Specifications

We are a group of electrical engineering seniors who have worked to design a knee stretch monitor for use by postoperative knee surgery patients and those suffering from knee or knee-related injuries. The goal of this device is to allow the patient to monitor his or her knee stretches, as well as to provide feedback to the user through a graphical user interface (GUI) detailing his or her progress, while motivating the patient through alerts to continue stretching in order to avoid more costly surgeries. However, this device is only meant to be used to monitor certain types of knee stretches that primarily consist of measuring the angle of the knee during bending and/or the level of the leg during leg raise exercises. The device will not be used to transmit patient data to third parties, such as the patient's medical provider or physical therapist in order to comply with HIPAA regulations.
 
Disclaimer: This project is currently in progress.
